# 💰 Business Case: Emuna OS 2.0 - ROI Analysis
**Prepared for:** Hospital Management (Sheba / Shalvata / Abarbanel)
**Subject:** Economic Justification for Implementing the "Security System for the Soul"

## 1. The Executive Problem
Current psychiatric care is **reactive**. Staff intervenes *after* an incident occurs, leading to high resource consumption, staff burnout, and long hospitalization cycles.

## 2. ROI Pillar 1: Incident Prevention
*   **Current Cost:** A single violent incident requires 4 security guards, 2 nurses, a physician, and a seclusion room. Estimated cost: **₪1,200 per event**.
*   **The Emuna Solution:** "The Anchor" identifies physiological stress 15 minutes before escalation.
*   **Projected Savings:** Preventing 10 incidents a month per ward = **₪144,000 annually**.

## 3. ROI Pillar 2: Readmission ("The Revolving Door")
*   **Current Cost:** Average 14-day readmission cost: **₪35,000**.
*   **The Emuna Solution:** Patients discharge with the "Cognitive Alchemist" in their pocket, providing 24/7 grounding.
*   **Projected Savings:** Reducing readmission rate by 15% = **₪420,000 annually** (for every 80 patients).

## 4. ROI Pillar 3: Staff Efficiency & Retention
*   **Current Pain:** Nurses spend ~4 hours daily on manual documentation and incident management.
*   **The Emuna Solution:** "The Sentinel" dashboard automates monitoring. Staff turnover costs (hiring/training) are ~₪50,000 per nurse.
*   **Projected Savings:** Saving 2 hours of staff time daily + reduced turnover = **₪90,000 annually**.

The "Security System for the Soul" pays for itself within **4 months** of operation. It is not just a clinical tool; it is a financial stability engine for the modern psychiatric hospital.
